Discover what all the buzz is about!

One of the major issues in the country recent years has been the rapid reduction in the number of farmers. Thousands of farmers are quitting farming everyday. One of the key reasons for it is the facilities and prices offered to the farmers for thier crops which is insufficient for the survival of the farmers. Thus, to ensure his survival a farmer is making his switch from farming to other servical jobs.

The government has been trying all possible methods to ensure farmers don't quit.In order to do that they have managed to provide various policies which helped farmers in effective facilities and reasonable prices for the crops. The problem with the approach though is the vendors responsible for the governing of the policy.

Instead of providing the sanctioned amount to the farmers, the vendors tend to provide only a part of it and the rest of the money goes into the vendor's pocket. This leaves the farmers with unreasonable amount even after a lot of involved hardwork.

Thus in order to solve the problem, Smart farming provides an approach to provide transparency and security between the transactions involving farmers and government. The technology involves usage of block chain where transactions can be treated as blocks and as it can't be tampered making it is easier to the government and farmers.

The approach solves two major issues

  • The transactions removes involvement of third party vendors and transactions remain between government agencies and the users.
  • Since a third party involvement is barred, it improves the architecture of the policy and saves an adequate amount of funds.
